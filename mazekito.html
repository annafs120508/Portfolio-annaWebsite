Mazekito

Project Overview

Mazekito is a 2D game which challenge the players to complete the labyrinth to reach the goal, that is feed pet. The players will take role as the animal, by using tilt controller to move the character in complete the challenges in the labyrinth. It completed with some characters and labyrinth sizes, gives flexibility and excitement in every session in the game.

Role in the Project

I am the programmer, responsible to develop the tilt controller, organizing the game's UI, and implementing buttons.
