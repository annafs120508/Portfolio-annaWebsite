3D Garuda the Protector

Project Overview

3D Garuda The Protector is a puzzle game with an adventure genre that invites players to explore a dungeon. In this game, players will take role of Garda, the reincarnation of Garuda, to find the antidote of a curse hidden within a trap-filled cave. With stunning 3D graphics, 3D Garuda The Protector offers an exciting and challenging gameplay experience.

Role in the Project

In this project, I am responsible for develop gameplay mechanics such as character movement, creating traps, and designing and organizing the map from 2D to 3D.
